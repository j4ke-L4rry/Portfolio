MANGA

City of Tagbilaran
Province of Bohol

Manga is one of the barangays in Tagbilaran City, Bohol. Based on the 2020 Census, it had a population of 7,552, accounting for 7.19% of the city’s total population.

Households

According to the 2015 Census, Manga had a household population of 7,204, distributed across 1,531 households, with an average of 4.71 members per household.

Historical Population

Manga’s population rose from 3,302 in 1990 to 7,552 in 2020, marking an increase of 4,250 residents over three decades. The 2020 Census further recorded a positive annual growth rate of 0.94%, reflecting an increase of 328 people compared to the 2015 figure of 7,224.
