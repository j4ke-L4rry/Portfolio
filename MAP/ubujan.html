UBUJAN

City of Tagbilaran
Province of Bohol

Ubujan is a barangay located in Tagbilaran City, Bohol. According to the 2020 Census, it had a population of 5,561, accounting for 5.30% of Tagbilaran’s total population.

Households

In the 2015 Census, Ubujan recorded a household population of 5,558, distributed across 1,073 households, with an average of 5.18 members per household.

Historical population

Ubujan’s population increased from 1,900 in 1990 to 5,561 in 2020, reflecting a growth of 3,661 people over three decades. However, the 2020 Census also recorded a slight decline compared to 2015, with a negative annual growth rate of 0.05% — equivalent to a decrease of 13 individuals from the previous figure of 5,574.
